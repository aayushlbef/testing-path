Challenge Rules & Tips

Official Rules

  • Must log steps daily before midnight
  • Use any tracking method (app, watch, pedometer)
  • 10,000 steps minimum for daily credit
  • Complete 25/30 days to qualify for prizes
  • Winners announced April 1st

Tips for Success

  • Park farther away from campus buildings
  • Take walking study breaks every hour
  • Walk to campus instead of driving when possible
  • Join our group walks on weekends
  • Find an accountability partner
