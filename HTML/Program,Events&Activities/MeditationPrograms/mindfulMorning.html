10-Minute Session Flow

1

Arrival & Settling (2 min)

Find a comfortable seated position, close your eyes, and begin to notice your breath.

2

Breath Awareness (3 min)

Guided focus on the natural rhythm of your breath, observing without changing.

3

Body Scan (2 min)

Gentle awareness of physical sensations from head to toe, releasing tension.

4

Intention Setting (2 min)

Set a positive intention for your day ahead with guided visualization.

5

Closing (1 min)

Gradual return to awareness, gentle opening of eyes, carrying calm with you.

Benefits of Daily Practice

Mental Clarity

Improved focus and concentration for better academic performance and decision-making.

Stress Reduction

Lower cortisol levels and reduced anxiety through regular mindfulness practice.

Emotional Balance

Better emotional regulation and increased resilience in facing daily challenges.

What to Expect

  • No Experience Needed

    Perfect for beginners and experienced meditators alike. All guidance is provided.

  • Quiet, Peaceful Environment

    Dimmed lights, optional cushions or chairs, and a distraction-free space.

  • Guided by Trained Facilitator

    Led by a certified mindfulness instructor with experience working with students.

  • Drop-In Friendly

    Come whenever you can - no commitment required. Sessions are the same format daily.

  • Optional Take-Home Resources

    Audio recordings and guided meditation apps recommended for continued practice.
