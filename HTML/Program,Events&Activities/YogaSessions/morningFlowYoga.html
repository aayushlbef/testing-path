Morning Flow Yoga

Start your day with energizing sun salutations and gentle stretches to awaken your body and mind. Perfect for students looking to boost energy and focus before classes.

Every Sunday, 7:00 AM - 8:00 AM
Maitidevi PlayGround
Average 15-20 participants per session
Ongoing - Join anytime
Register for This Session

What to Expect

Session Structure

  • 5 minutes: Centering & breathing exercises
  • 20 minutes: Sun salutations & standing poses
  • 20 minutes: Balance work & gentle flows
  • 10 minutes: Cool down & stretching
  • 5 minutes: Final relaxation (Savasana)

Benefits

  • Increased energy and mental clarity
  • Improved flexibility and strength
  • Better posture and body awareness
  • Stress reduction and improved focus
  • Community connection with fellow students

What to Bring

Required

  • Comfortable workout clothing
  • Water bottle
  • Positive attitude!

Optional

  • Personal yoga mat (mats provided)
  • Towel
  • Yoga blocks or props

Note: Yoga mats and props are provided free of charge. Please arrive 5-10 minutes early for your first session to complete a brief health questionnaire.
