Common Questions

What are your office hours?

Our office is open Monday through Friday from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. You can drop by anytime during these hours - no appointment needed!

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all emails and messages within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call us directly.

Can I schedule a one-on-one meeting?

Absolutely! Email us or call to schedule a personal consultation with one of our wellness coordinators or student leaders.

Do I need to be a member to contact you?

Not at all! We welcome questions and inquiries from all students, whether you're a member, considering joining, or just curious about our programs.
