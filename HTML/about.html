About Our Organization

Our Mission

To empower students with the knowledge, tools, and support they need to lead balanced, healthy lives while navigating the challenges of college. We strive to create an inclusive environment where every student feels valued and supported on their wellness journey.

Our Vision

To be the leading student organization championing holistic wellness on campus, creating a culture where health and well-being are prioritized, celebrated, and accessible to all students regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Story

The Health and Wellness Society was founded in 2025 by a group of passionate students who recognized a gap in accessible wellness resources on campus. What started as informal study groups focused on stress management has grown into a thriving community of over 100 junior members.

Our journey began when our founder, Aayush Gupta, experienced firsthand the challenges of maintaining physical and mental well-being during the demanding college years. He envisioned a student-led organization that would provide peer support, evidence-based resources, and engaging programs to help fellow students thrive.

Today, we're proud to offer a comprehensive range of programs including fitness classes, mental health workshops, nutrition counseling, and community events. Our organization has become a vital part of campus life, creating a supportive environment where students can prioritize their health and connect with others on similar wellness journeys.
