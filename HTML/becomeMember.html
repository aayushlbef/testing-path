Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a membership fee?

No! Membership is completely free for all enrolled students. We believe wellness should be accessible to everyone.

Do I need to attend every meeting?

Not at all! Attend events and meetings that fit your schedule. We understand students are busy and encourage participation at your own pace.

What if I'm new to health and wellness?

Perfect! Our community welcomes students at all levels. We provide beginner-friendly resources and supportive mentors to help you get started.

Can I get involved in leadership?

Absolutely! We have various leadership positions and committee opportunities. After joining, you can express your interest in taking on leadership roles.
